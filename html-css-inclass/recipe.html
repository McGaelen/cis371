Flavored Chicken

Featured in Asian Recipes, Indonesian Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 2 table spoons curry powder
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 4 tablespoon chopped coriander root and stem
  • 1 kg chicken breast fillets, cut into 2cm pieces

Method

  1. Place coconut cream, curry, garlic, sugar, fish sauce and coriander in a blender and process until smooth Place chicken in a non-metallic bowl, add marinade and stir well. Cover and refrigerate overnight. The next day, thread 3-4 pieces of chick on each skewer
  2. Preheat grill to high, grill 5-7 minutes, turning, until cooked through. Serve on platters with mango chutney, if desired.

Equipments

You will need 30 wooden skewers, soaked for 1 hour in cold water. Begin this recipe the day before.
